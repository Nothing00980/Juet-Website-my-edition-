Our Facilities

At Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology, we take pride in providing a conducive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, research innovation, and overall development. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance the educational journey and enrich the student experience.

Learning Resource Center

The Learning Resource Center of JUET plays very important & vital role in all academic & research activities.It is fully computerized by a web based Library management software "Liberty". Users can search their desired information at their own desktop such as availability of books, current status of their account, new arrivals.. .etc through web OPAC ( Online Public Access Catalogue). At present LRC is having good collection of reference sources like hand book, standard, manuals, encyclopedia, technical reports, project reports along with the prescribed text and reference books, and national & International printed journals.

Hostel

The University has separate hostel facilities for boys and girls. Presently, there are 21 blocks of boy's hostels having capacity to accommodate 1830 students. The girls hostel is having capacity to accommodate around 425 inmates with its own sporting & messing facility.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Separate Annapurnas have already been established for Boys and Girls. These provide dining facilities to students and employees. These have a modern steam operated kitchen to ensure adequate hygiene and an automated Chapati making machine. The dinning hall is equipped with dining tables to accommodate around 500 students at a time in three dinning halls. A cafeteria equipped with a modern hygienic kitchen is also operational in Annapurna complex.

Large PlayGround

Great stress is laid on students personality development. For this reason besides the Indoor Games and Gymnasium facilities, large playing and recreational complex is being developed. Already Basket Ball, Cricket, Volley Ball, Tennis, Football and Badminton facilities are functional. A stadium, Swimming Pool and Open Air Theatre are planned. The recreational facilities when complete will compare with the best facilities in any technical education.

Gymnasium

Holistic good health is an asset of everyone! The health corner of JUET is equipped with three well equipped Gyms mainly comprise with heavy weight and light equipments. Gyms are endowed with home gym, eight station Gym, all types of benches, power stepper, magnetic jogger, weight lifting kits, cable cross, twister, dumbles, exercise bike, absleemer, floor type weighing scale, etc.

Tagore Hall

Multipurpose hall with following facility :

Centrally air cooled
Seating capacity 2200 with multiple entries and exits
Audio Visual facility
Multi screen stage of three layer screen room.
Playing arena for three badminton courts / one basket ball court / three table tennis tables.

Health Care Center

The University has a Health Care Center inside the campus. It provides the medical facility to its residents. It comprises of one experienced doctor, two compounders and one pathologist. The university owns two ambulances for 24 hrs. outdoor facilities. Various demonstrations in first-aid and Cardio-pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR), are available to the students and other staff of the university.

ATM

An extension counter of The Oriental Bank of Commerce and Axis Bank is inside the Campus which deals with the banking facility of the students as well as the university. An ATM counter of Oriental Bank of Commerce has been established at the main entrance of the university.

Saftey

University has a well euipped team of professionals to handle the cases of fire. Fire fighting devices have been installed on all suitable locations. University also owns well equipped fire fighting vehicle.
